Dedicated to the DIY nature of the Web

Do you create tools, sites, projects, apps or anything else on the web for non-business purposes? If so, you're part of a long line of amazing developers and designers that made the Web an amazing place to experiment. At webMade, we believe in this DIY philosophy. Not everything on the Web needs to be a business. In this magazine, we'll highlight methods, techniques and projects for you to build your skills with things that will serve a purpose for you.
