Dedicated to the DIY nature of the Web

Coming Late 2019

Do you create tools, sites, projects, apps or anything else on the web for non-business purposes? If so, you're part of a long line of amazing developers and designers that made the Web a great place to experiment.

In this magazine, we'll have monthly articles helping you explore a DIY philosophy on the web. Not everything needs to be a business. We'll highlight methods, techniques, and projects for you to build your skills and make things that matter to you.

The Current Outline for Issue 1:

  • Feature: The DIY Web: Where we came from, where we went wrong, where we're going
  • Letter from the Editor: Welcome to a new concept
  • Project Tutorial: (TBD) A step-by-step article building a usefule, fun project
  • Education: (TBD) An article explaining a computer science concept or a programming or language feature
  • Project Prompts: A set of 5 projects to build with inspiration, specifications and hints
